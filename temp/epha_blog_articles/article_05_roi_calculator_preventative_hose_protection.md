## Part 1: Understanding ROI in Preventative Maintenance

### The Traditional ROI Calculation

**Standard Formula:**
```
ROI % = [(Gain from Investment - Cost of Investment) / Cost of Investment] × 100
```

**Applied to hose protection:**
```
ROI % = [(Annual Savings - Protection Cost) / Protection Cost] × 100
```

**Example (50-vehicle fleet):**
- Annual savings: $464,640
- Protection cost: $7,500
- ROI = [($464,640 - $7,500) / $7,500] × 100 = **6,095%**
