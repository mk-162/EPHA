**Step 2: Prioritize High-Risk Equipment (Week 2-3)**

Not all equipment has equal failure risk. Focus protection on:

**High Priority:**
- Equipment with history of repeated failures
- Vehicles in harsh operating conditions (extreme temps, high vibration, chemical exposure)
- Critical equipment where downtime costs exceed $1,000/day
- Long-haul or remote operation equipment (expensive emergency service)

**Medium Priority:**
- Standard fleet vehicles in normal conditions
- Backup equipment with lower utilization
- Equipment with moderate downtime costs ($300-$1,000/day)

**Lower Priority:**
- Spare equipment rarely used
- Equipment scheduled for replacement within 12 months
- Low-downtime-cost equipment
