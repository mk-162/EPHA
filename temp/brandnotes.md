EPHA Hose Protectors represent a high-value solution in the growing $11.99 billion industrial hose market (2025), projected to reach $13.92 billion by 2030 with a 3.0% CAGR. These durable wear shields protect critical hydraulic lines, cables, and hoses from abrasion, chemicals, and extreme temperatures, preventing costly downtime and equipment failure across industrial, automotive, construction, and transportation sectors.

**Key Market Opportunity:** With preventative maintenance reducing downtime by up to 85% and hydraulic hose failures costing thousands in repairs and lost productivity, EPHA’s cost-effective solution ($3.90-$10.70 per unit) addresses a critical pain point for fleet maintenance, manufacturing, construction, and industrial MRO markets.
